The Lifeboat

An argumentation activity

The situation: An ocean liner has struck an iceberg and is rapidly sinking. One lifeboat remains. It can hold seven people — but ten are hoping to board. Read each passenger's profile carefully, decide who gets a seat and who must stay behind, and write a brief reason for every decision. Be prepared to defend your choices in discussion.
